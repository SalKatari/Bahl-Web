About me

She has an unsurpassable combination of experience- teaching at a large diverse High School In USA, Curriculum and content builder for school districts in US and with NCERT in India. For the last 7 years, she has switched to virtual media- using technology and her expertise to coach students from all over the world in one to one coaching through virtual learning- all from the flexibility of their home at their time.

Do you want to achieve your best?

You want to prepare for the coveted SAT/ACT exam with a coach who offers a unique combination of knowledge, life coaching skills and unparalleled attention and commitment to help bring out the best in you.

I follow a nontraditional approach - Using virtual learning, focusing on an active mode learning, in tandem with your speed of learning-all while integrating my long international teaching experience and life coaching skills. To help the learner achieve his/her best, I adapt to his/her capabilities by running a diagnostic at the start to assess the areas of strength and weakness and then curating the lessons and practice accordingly.
